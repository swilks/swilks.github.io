Welcome!

Hey! Welcome to my website. I’m a passionate software engineer, intrepid start-up director and budding product designer, currently working in e-health leading the dev team at Maisha Meds. I’m driven by the desire to engineer sustainable, impactful change through technology. I’m relentlessly curious, always in search of new experiences, and pretty much always down for an adventure. My background is in software engineering and business, and I’ve developed a love for design and a keen interest in public health through my work at Maisha Meds.

About Me

Professionally I am driven, versatile and creative. I am passionate about finding solutions to real problems, and doing work that leads to quantifiable improvements in people's lives. I’ve designed, built and deployed software that aims to improve primary care across East Africa, worked on apps for small start-ups and established giants, and am currently taking full advantage of the opportunity to help build a company.

Personally Personally I’ve always had a penchant for adventure, have developed a keen interest in photography and love to both eat and cook (plus I’m nurturing a minor coffee addiction). I’m constantly trying to push myself out of my comfort zone, learn new things and meet new and interesting people. East Africa has provided me with an amazing opportunity to explore and learn in a culturally rich and diverse region.

Some Things I'm good at

Software Engineering

During my professional career I have developed two production apps from scratch for award-winning companies, built a product suite from the ground up, worked on established products for Silicon Valley Giants, and contributed to major releases for growth-stage companies as both a developer and product manager

Product Design and Management

Designing Maisha Meds’ Android App for rural Kenyan pharmacists – a demographic that is largely unfamiliar with technology - has been one of the most challenging and rewarding jobs I’ve ever had. It has allowed to implement and refine the skills I picked up as a product manager for Scribd, and the tools I gained academically.

Business and Leadership

Through my experience as an early employee at Maisha Meds , as well as an academic background in business administration, I’ve developed strong leadership and management skills. Helping to start and run a seed-stage startup has given me an in-depth understanding of the challenges involved in starting and running a business.
